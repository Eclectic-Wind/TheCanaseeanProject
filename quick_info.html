Welcome to Canaseëa

Welcome to Canaseëa and the greater world of Idril. This is a worldbuilding project managed by Kam, focusing on a Victorian-era magic steampunk setting brimming with intricate politics, diverse cultures, complex economics, and, of course, a unique magic system!

The World's Geography

Idril features broadly 9 land continents (or 8, if Canaseea is treated as a singular entity):

  • Canaseëa (often divided into Western and Eastern regions)
  • The Dulvan Continent (comprising various component states)
  • The Kayin Archipelagos
  • Asdent (sometimes referred to as The New World)
  • Other continents (details pending further development)

The Magic System: Semeiographia

The world is distinguished by its extremely strict, hard magic system known as Semeiographia. In this system, science, engineering, magic, and philosophy intertwine. It relies on intricate glyphs and circles that interconnect to create complex and predictable magical effects.
